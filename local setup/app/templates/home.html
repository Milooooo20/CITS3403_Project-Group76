About R-echo




Wanting to know more of your music taste?


Looking for new songs?


R-echo is here for you to use!




By submitting as simple as one song, or a personal playlist, R-echo can analyse the songs you listen to and give you insights to your own unique music taste.


Know what genres you tend to listen to, discover new songs from our recommendations that will expand your music-world.


You can also share your results with other R-echo users, comparing and contrasting your music with others. See if you could find similarities or differences in the songs you and others listen to!



R-echo can be summarised in 3 words:


Submit. Suggest. Share.
